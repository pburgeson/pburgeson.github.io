Cable CNC

Creating a 2 axis CNC machine that utilizes a cable system instead of standard linear axes. The basis of this idea is the Maslow CNC The intent is to make a 2 axis machine cablable of at least plywood CNC'ing and CNC plasma cutting of sheet metal. The motors will be on the carriage to put all of the complexity into a single location and to allow for futuring scaling into a 3 axis cable CNC for use in a garden.

High Level Requirements

  1. Mechanics (no tool) < $750
  2. Accurate to +/- 0.010" on X and Y
  3. Automated Z lift accurate to +/- 0.025"
  4. Swappable tools for wood router, plasma, laser pen
  5. Vacuum system for wood and laser

Other more detailed requirements will be created as the project develops further.
